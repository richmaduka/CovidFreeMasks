Human-friendly guidelines

The website will not sell your data nor use any of the information except for matching together people who want to donate masks, and healthcare workers who need them. All data will be deleted after this is all over. There is one and only one purpose to the site, which is to help our healthcare workers who are on the front lines and are now facing personally dangerous conditions due to the severe shortage and hospital PPE rationing. These guidelines dictate the spirit and operations of freecovid19masks and how we handle your data. However the legally-binding, yet generic, privacy policy is included below.
