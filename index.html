Mask Types

Homemade Masks

CDC advises the use of simple cloth face coverings to slow the spread of the virus. Our mission is to ensure that every individual, household, place of worship, ER, urgent care, doctor's office, small office or retail shop that wants a good quality mask has one.

Learn how to make masks
Respirators and Surgical Masks

An N95 respirator is a respiratory protective device designed to achieve a very close facial fit and very efficient filtration of airborne particles. The 'N95' designation means that when subjected to careful testing, the respirator blocks at least 95 percent of very small (0.3 micron) test particles. A surgical mask is a loose-fitting, disposable device that creates a physical barrier between the mouth and nose of the wearer and potential contaminants in the immediate environment.

How It Works

We match donors to people in need.

If you need masks, fill out the I need masks form so we know who you are and where your donor needs to send your masks.

If you have masks, fill out the I have masks to give form and we will email you with the information of a match so that you can ship your masks directly to them. Donor will inform the recipient on the best or most convenient delivery method.

Our team verifies the identities of both the recipient and the donor.

Who are we?

Nwamaka Nnamani

I am an anesthesiologist based in Dallas, Texas. I started outsourcing mask production to local designers on March 9th 2020 and donating them to healthcare facilities, food banks and shelters. When the CDC recommended that masks are worn in public areas, I decided to create this site to get nationwide momentum with the purpose of the public creating and donating masks to their local doctor’s offices, emergency rooms, ER, grocery stores etc to ensure that masks are available for all even those that cannot afford one.
